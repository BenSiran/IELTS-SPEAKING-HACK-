Quick Practice

Part 1: Introduction & Interview

Part 1 lasts 4-5 minutes. The examiner will ask general questions about yourself and familiar topics.

üè† Home & Accommodation

Do you live in a house or an apartment?
I currently live in a spacious apartment on the outskirts of the city. It's a modern two-bedroom flat that I share with my roommate.
What's your favorite room in your home?
My favorite room would definitely be the living room. It's where I spend most of my leisure time, whether I'm reading, watching movies, or entertaining guests.
Part 2: Individual Long Turn

Part 2 lasts 3-4 minutes. You'll receive a task card and have 1 minute to prepare, then speak for 2 minutes.

üéØ Cue Card Topic 1

Describe a person who has influenced you
I'd like to talk about my high school mathematics teacher, Mrs. Patterson, who has had a profound influence on my life and career path.
Part 3: Discussion

Part 3 lasts 4-5 minutes and discusses abstract ideas related to the Part 2 topic.

üí° General Questions about Influence
