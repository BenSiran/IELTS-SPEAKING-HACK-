Part 1: Introduction & Interview

Part 1 lasts 4-5 minutes. The examiner will ask general questions about yourself and familiar topics.

SAMPLE ANSWER:

I currently live in a spacious apartment on the outskirts of the city. It's a modern two-bedroom flat that I share with my roommate. What I particularly appreciate about it is the abundance of natural light, thanks to the large windows in the living room. The location is quite convenient as well, with easy access to public transportation and local amenities.

Q: What's your favorite room in your home?
SAMPLE ANSWER:

My favorite room would definitely be the living room. It's where I spend most of my leisure time, whether I'm reading, watching movies, or entertaining guests. I've decorated it with comfortable furniture and some personal touches like family photos and plants, which create a warm and welcoming atmosphere. It's essentially the heart of my home.

I'm particularly interested in artificial intelligence and machine learning, which I hope to specialize in.

Q: Why did you choose this field?

The field offers endless opportunities for innovation and creativity, which really appeals to me. Plus, the job prospects are excellent, which was certainly a practical consideration.
